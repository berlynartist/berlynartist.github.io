Berlyn Omejia

Artist & Painter

Berlyn Chima Omejia is a self taught artist. At the tender age of 5 years old, Berlyn along with her family escaped from the war torn African Nation of Liberia. Her family's courage and pride are displayed in her artwork which she began creating in her late teen years with no instruction. Berlyn attributes her talent as a gift from God. Berlyn has completed dozens of portraits while pursuing a degree in Art. She currently works as a Free Lance artist, painting and creating for a large and growing clientele all over the Dallas ForthWorth Metroplex.
